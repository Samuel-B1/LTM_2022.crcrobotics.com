Our Team

-

In 2011 our school began participating in the CRC competition. Although there were only three members at that time, more and more people became interested each year, and now we have over 20 members on our team cooperating together to participate in this year’s competition.

Tasks

Our Robotics team is composed of many different subgroups, each is equally important. Our team is composed of members that work on the:
Kiosk, where the members there focus on creating the background for the robot.
Film making, where we create the video on our robot and its theme.
Robot building team, where the robot is created.
Robot designing, in which the robot would not exist without.
Website team, where all the information about us exists.
Journalism, where all the information needed for the website is gathered.
It’s thanks to all these members in every subgroup that our Robotics team has creative and innovative ideas for this year’s competition.

Our Mentors


This past year has been particularly tough. Even so, our team has helped in any way we could. The students here are dedicated and ambitious. We are very happy to learn about building and designing robots, coding websites, making kiosks, and filming. Every class we spent in Robotics, our members were constantly doing something educational and fun. Most of us didn’t know the first thing about coding or building robots before we entered the Robotics club. All members are extremely grateful to have this extra curricular activity available to us.
